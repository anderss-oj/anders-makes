beluchaga

2022. The word beluchaga originates from me. It is a nonsense word I made to try and fit most of the unique letterform elements when trying to brainstorm typeface forms. The typeface went through many blocky, heavy, and soft forms before landing on this smooth but not squishy form. In this spiral bound specimen, I create a small dictionary to hold other nonsensical, made up words, with their own definitions.
